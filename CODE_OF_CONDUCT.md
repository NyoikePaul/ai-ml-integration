# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Committed to welcoming community for all.

## Our Standards

Good:
- Respectful of opinions
- Accepting feedback
- Inclusive language
- Patient with newcomers

Bad:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Publishing private info
- Unwelcome advances

## Enforcement

Leaders enforce standards.

Report to: conduct@nyoikepaul.dev

Consequences:
- First: Warning + conversation
- Serious: Temporary ban
- Repeated: Permanent ban
